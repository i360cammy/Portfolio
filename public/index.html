About Me

I am motivated by the belief that data is the key to solving many real-world problems. From forecasting hurricane tracks to improved allocation of resources for relief efforts. Correct processing of observations into actionable information is crucial.

My journey into Data Science started with a desire to understand the world around me. Data Science provided a way to make sense of complex systems and make predictions about the future.

I graduated from the University of London in 2022 with a First Class Honours BSc in Computer Science, and in 2024 from the University of Aberdeen with an MSc with Distinction in Data Science
